🚀 WizLingo Beta Roadmap

100 Warm Users • 8 Weeks • Closed Beta

📋 Phase 1: Setup (Week 1)

Distribution Method (Pick One)

Option A: Web App (Easiest)

  • Deploy the app to a web URL: beta.wizlingo.app or beta.edvanta.in
  • Share the link via email: "Here's your beta access → [link]"
  • No app store, no TestFlight, no APK mess
  • Works on all devices instantly
  • Best if: Your app is web-based (Next.js, React)

Option B: TestFlight (iOS) + Google Play Closed Beta (Android)

  • iOS: Use Apple TestFlight (up to 100 testers, link-based)
  • Android: Google Play → "Internal testing" track (link-based)
  • More official, but requires app store setup
  • Best if: You want real app binaries on phones

Option C: Direct APK (Android) + Web (iOS)

  • Android: Generate APK, share via Google Drive/Dropbox link
  • iOS: Share web app link
  • Hybrid, simplest for fast iteration
✨ Recommendation: Web App + TestFlight
Web app: Main version, everyone uses this (easiest)
TestFlight: For iOS power users who want native feel
Single source of truth: One codebase, deploy once, both platforms work

💡 Key Insights

Why 100 Warm Users is Perfect

  • They'll actually use it (not abandon after 1 day)
  • They'll give detailed feedback (not just ⭐⭐⭐)
  • You can iterate fast (smaller group = quicker feedback loops)
  • They'll share bugs naturally (invested in your success)
  • Lower stress (no public reputation on the line yet)

Biggest Risk & Mitigation

⚠️ Risk: Low DAU means hard to spot bugs

Mitigation:
  • Weekly email reminding them to test
  • Celebrate wins ("Rahul earned 10 badges!")
  • Quick bug fixes (24 hours max)
