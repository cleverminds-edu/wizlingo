⚠️ Risk Mitigation

If School Commits BUT Beta Metrics Are Weak

Scenario: School A agrees to pilot, but your DAU is only 40% (below target of 70%)

Action: Be honest. "Our beta showed some engagement challenges we're fixing. Rather than launch June 15 with imperfect product, let's launch June 20 after we tune difficulty." Schools respect honesty + transparency. This builds trust for renewal.

If Teacher Adoption Is Low

Scenario: June 15 launch: only 20% of teachers assigned homework

Action: (1) Call each teacher personally, "Did you get our guide? Any questions?" (2) Simplify assignment UI if feedback is "too complex" (3) Do in-person training June 16-17 at each school

If Speech Rec Accuracy Drops

Scenario: June 15 launch: speech rec only 78% accurate (below 85% target)

Action: (1) Pause VOICE_WIZARD badge (speaking) temporarily (2) Focus on WORD_WIZARD (reading, no speech rec) (3) Collect accent/dialect samples, retrain model June 16-20 (4) Relaunch speaking June 22

If Logistics Fails (Credentials Not Printed)

Scenario: June 15 morning: credential cards not at School B yet

Action: (1) Email login links directly to students (if emails available) (2) SMS links to parent phones (if available) (3) Schools print QR codes with login info, post in class (4) Launch with 90% of students instead of waiting for all cards
