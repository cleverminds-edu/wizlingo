# WizLingo: Go-to-Market Strategy (One-Pager)
## The Path to ₹25Cr+ ARR in 3 Years

**Prepared for:** Board, Investors, Leadership Team  
**Date:** June 2026  
**Next milestone:** Stage 1 completion (August 31, 2026)  
**Series A target:** Q4 2026 / Q1 2027

---

## The Opportunity

**Market:** Indian K-12 English language skills (260M students)  
**Product:** AI-powered reading + speaking platform  
**Model:** B2B school subscriptions (white-labeled, co-branded)  
**TAM (Year 1):** Schools in Tier 1/2 cities willing to pay ₹1-5L/year  

---

## Three-Stage Go-to-Market Plan

### **STAGE 1: Beta Testing (June-August 2026)**
**100-150 free students → Validate product & metrics**
- Recruit 2-3 schools with 100+ students (free access)
- Measure: Engagement (DAU >70%), Badge earn rate (>30%), Parent email opens (>25%)
- Validate: Speech recognition accuracy (>90%), Teacher adoption (if assigned)
- **Outcome:** Green gates = proof product works before charging

**Success = 4/5 metrics pass by August 31**

---

### **STAGE 2: Paid Pilots (Sept-Dec 2026)**
**2 schools × 2000+ students → Prove school-market fit**
- Launch 2 schools at 75% discount (₹50K/school = ₹37.5K below cost)
- Measure: Teacher assignment rate (>60%), Student retention (>70%), Churn rate (<10%)
- Get: Case studies, testimonials, teacher feedback
- **Outcome:** If schools renew at 50%+ full price = ready to scale

**Success = Both schools want Year 2 at ≥50% full price**

---

### **STAGE 3: Scale & Series A (Jan-June 2027)**
**15-20 schools → ₹1-2Cr ARR → Raise Series A**
- Roll out tier-based pricing (₹100-₹400/student/year by city)
- Hire BD team (2-3 people) to target 15+ schools
- Use case studies to unlock partnerships (state boards, teacher associations)
- **Outcome:** ₹1-2Cr ARR, proven unit economics, ready for Series A

**Success = ₹1-2Cr ARR by June 2027**

---

## Pricing Strategy

### **Model: Per-Student Annual**

```
Tier 1 (Elite metro schools): ₹400/student/year
  Example: 500-student school = ₹2L/year

Tier 1.5 (Good metro schools): ₹250/student/year
  Example: 1000-student school = ₹2.5L/year

Tier 2 (Tier-2 cities): ₹150/student/year
  Example: 1000-student school = ₹1.5L/year

Tier 3 (Tier-3, aspirational, volume): ₹100/student/year
  Example: 1000-student school = ₹1L/year
```

**Why this model:**
✅ Simple (schools understand it like BYJU'S)  
✅ Scalable (bigger schools = bigger revenue)  
✅ Fair (pay proportional to value)  
✅ Investor-friendly (linear ARR growth story)

## Competitive Advantages

| Advantage | How We Win |
|---|---|
| **Speech recognition** | Better accuracy than free APIs (Whisper), integrated into school workflow |
| **Gamification** | Badge system drives habit formation + parent sharing (not just boring progress) |
| **School integration** | White-labeled, co-branded certificates, teacher dashboards (not a standalone app) |
| **TAM clarity** | B2B schools > B2C parents (predictable revenue, institutional buyers) |
| **First-mover in India** | Vedantu/BYJU'S have reading modules but not dedicated learning platform |

---

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Speech rec accuracy <90%** | Validate on 20 recordings by June 20, have backup API (OpenAI Whisper) |
| **Teachers don't adopt** | Conduct 5+ teacher interviews in Stage 1, iterate based on feedback |
| **Schools won't pay** | Start with 75% discount pilots, data drives renewal pricing |
| **Competitor enters** | Focus on teacher experience (not student), defensible because embedded in school workflow |
| **Churn after Year 1** | Build retention features (teacher reports, parent engagement, leaderboards) |

## Why This Works

1. **B2B school model = stable revenue** (vs. B2C which is highly competitive)
2. **High unit economics** (LTV:CAC >20:1 targets)
3. **Clear expansion path** (150 schools in Tier 1-2 cities = ₹50Cr+ ARR)
4. **Defensible product** (speech recognition is hard to replicate)
5. **Massive TAM** (260M students, even 5% = ₹100Cr+ market)
6. **First-mover advantage** (no pure play competitor in India yet)
