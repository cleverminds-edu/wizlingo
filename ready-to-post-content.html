TIP #2: Write Badly First

Your biggest mistake:
Waiting to write perfectly.

Here's what happens:
• You stare at blank page
• Pressure to be perfect
• Write nothing
• Procrastinate instead

Here's what winners do:
• Write terrible first draft

The secret:
Perfect writing comes from editing.

Your job = write terribly.

TIP #3: Learn 1 Word. Speak 5 Times.

Most students learn 50 words.
Remember 5.

Why? Because reading ≠ speaking.

This is how fluency happens.
Not from reading.
From active speaking.
