### **B2C Unit Economics**

```
Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): ₹50-200
├─ Organic (referrals): ₹0-50
├─ Parent groups (WhatsApp): ₹0
├─ School promotion: ₹0
└─ Paid ads (optional): ₹100-200

Lifetime Value (LTV):
├─ Average subscription: 12 months
├─ Monthly price: ₹235
├─ Total LTV: ₹235 × 12 = ₹2,820
├─ Gross margin: 85% = ₹2,397
└─ Net LTV (after support): ₹2,100

LTV:CAC Ratio: 2,100 ÷ 100 = 21:1 ✅ (Excellent)
Payback Period: 1-2 months (fast, sustainable)

```

├─ Sales materials: ₹5K
├─ Demo + training: ₹3K
└─ Travel: ₹0-2K

Lifetime Value:
├─ Initial deal: ₹147,500
├─ Renewal likelihood: 85% (strong teacher adoption)
├─ Repeat years: 2-3 years typical
├─ Total LTV (3-year): ₹147,500 × 2.5 = ₹368,750
├─ Gross margin: 92% = ₹338,750
└─ Net LTV (after CS): ₹300,000

LTV:CAC Ratio: 300,000 ÷ 18,000 = 16.7:1 ✅ (Excellent)
